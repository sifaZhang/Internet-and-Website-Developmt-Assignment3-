Waka Wai Rescue

Introduction

The purpose of this game is to inspire players to care for the precious waterways of Aotearoa New Zealand. Water (wai) is a taonga—a treasured resource deeply valued in Māori culture and across communities.

Your mission is to collect as much trash as possible within the time limit to help protect the rivers (awa) and seas (wai-tai). Use the arrow keys or W, A, S, D to steer your vessel. Press the spacebar to collect trash when your vessel intersects with it.

How to play:
W or ↑: Move to up;
S or ↓: Move to down;
A or ←: Move to left;
D or →: Move to right;
Space: Pick trashes;
Ctrl: Make a sprite.
